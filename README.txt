This a VR Game running only on windows PC

VR Headset : Meta Quest 2, Oculus Rift S, etc... (OpenXR headsets)

1. Connect any version of Meta headset (or any OpenXR headset) with connected controllers to the PC (Windows Only)
2. Make sure your headset is loaded completely. (Able to show PC desktop, everythign..)
2. Open Vampire Castle.exe to launch the game from your PC, but do not put on headset yet. (This is important)
3. Click on Start Game button by your mouse, from your PC, you should see a white ground scene from your PC.
4. Wear the headset from now.
5. You are ready to play!

0. In the game, you can press setting button on left controller to exit the game, back the menu, etc.
